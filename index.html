I-4 Ultimate, the largest highway infrastructure project in Florida history, is complete. Central Florida motorists now enjoy a smoother drive in the well-traveled corridor, which spans 21 miles of Interstate 4 (I-4) from west of Kirkman Road (State Road (S.R.) 435) to east of S.R. 434.

I-4 Ultimate included the construction of two tolled managed lanes in each direction, known locally as I-4 Express. These lanes in the center of I-4 offer motorists a new option for longer trips.

New I-4 improvements under construction in Orange and Osceola Counties are part of the Florida Department of Transportation’s (FDOT) I-4 Beyond the Ultimate project.

To report an emergency, dial 911. For non-emergency roadside assistance, dial *FHP (*347).
